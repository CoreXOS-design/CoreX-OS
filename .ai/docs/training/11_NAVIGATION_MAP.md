# 11 — Navigation Map (Where Do I Go To…?)

**Purpose.** A plain-language "where do I go to do X" map of CoreX. Ellie uses this
to answer navigation questions and hand you the exact link. Every direct link
below is a real, live page — click it or paste it after your CoreX address.

> Ellie also resolves these links live from the Navigation Atlas, filtered to the
> pages your role can open — so she never sends you somewhere you can't access.

---

## Presentations and CMAs

**Where do I go to create a new presentation for a property?**
You create a presentation from the property itself — there is no standalone
presentation builder. Go to Properties (`/corex/properties`), open the property you
want, and click "Generate Presentation".

To see presentations you've already made: Sidebar → Presentations (`/presentations`).
To see how recipients engaged: Presentations → Analytics. To record results:
Presentations → Outcomes.

---

## Properties and Listings

**Where do I go to add or manage a listing?**
Sidebar → **Properties** (My Listings). Direct link: `/corex/properties`
This is your agency stock — the properties you hold a mandate on. Add a property,
edit details, and manage status here.

For every property CoreX has intelligence on (not just your stock), use
**Prospecting → Tracked Properties / Canvassing**.

---

## Contacts

**Where do I go to add a contact / find a client?**
Sidebar → **Contacts**. Direct link: `/corex/contacts`
Owners, buyers, tenants, and landlords all live here. Add a contact, capture their
wishlist, and see their linked properties, deals, and communications.

---

## Market Intelligence and Matching

**Where do I go to match buyers to properties / see market intelligence?**
Sidebar → **Market Intelligence**. Direct link: `/corex/market-intelligence`
The MIC runs buyer↔property matching, market narratives, and suburb intelligence.
For the strongest matches surfaced automatically, use **Core Matches**.

---

## Buyers

**Where do I go to work my buyers?**
Sidebar → **Buyer Pipeline**. Direct link: `/corex/command-center/buyers/pipeline`
Track buyers from lead through matched to offer.

---

## Deals and Commission

**Where do I go to register a new deal / sale?**
Sidebar → **Deals** → **Create**. Direct link: `/deals-v2/create`
Capture a sale, rental, mandate, or offer. See all deals under **Deals**, and track
stages under **Deal Pipeline**.

**Where do I check my commission?**
Sidebar → **Commission**. View earnings, splits, and settlement.

---

## Documents and E-Signature

**Where do I go to create or sign a document?**
Sidebar → **DocuPerfect**. Start a new document from a template, or send one out for
signing. Start an e-signature directly at `/docuperfect/esign/create`.

**Where do I find a filed document?**
Sidebar → **Document Library**. Direct link: `/documents/library`
Everything filed against deals, contacts, and properties is searchable here.

---

## Compliance (FICA / PPRA / POPIA)

**Where do I go to do FICA on a contact?**
Sidebar → **Compliance → FICA**. Direct link: `/corex/compliance/fica`
Manage FICA verification and documents. Agent FFC/CPD status lives under
**Compliance → Agent Compliance**.

---

## Calendar and Tasks

**Where do I go to see my diary / appointments?**
Sidebar → **Calendar**. Direct link: `/corex/command-center/calendar`
Your to-dos and follow-ups are under **Tasks**.

---

## Rentals

**Where do I go for rentals and leases?**
Sidebar → **Rentals**. Manage rental listings, tenants, and leases. Current
agreements are under **Active Leases**.

---

## Payroll and Leave

**Where do I go to apply for leave?**
Sidebar → **Payroll & Leave → Leave**. Direct link: `/corex/payroll/leave/dashboard`
Apply for leave, view balances, and manage applications.

---

## Tools

**Where are the calculators and tools?**
Sidebar → **Tools**. Includes the CMA tool, bond/transfer calculators, the PDF Suite,
the image converter, and the Ad Manager (Property24 / Private Property syndication).

---

## Help, Training, and Ellie

**Where do I go to learn CoreX or get help?**
Sidebar → **Training** for courses, or **Help & Guides** for how-to references.
To ask Ellie anything, open **Ellie** from the sidebar or the chat widget.

---

## Admin and Settings

**Where do I go to change agency branding / settings?**
Sidebar → **Admin → Company Settings** (agency-wide branding, branches, config), or
**Settings** for your personal preferences.

**Where does Ellie's knowledge come from?**
Admin → **Knowledge Base**. Upload documents there and enable them for Ellie so she
can answer questions about them.
